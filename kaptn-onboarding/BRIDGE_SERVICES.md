**Original Service:** Your 24/7 AI assistant that understands your business, delivers real-time clarity, and helps you make smarter decisions every day.

**Bridge Name:** **COMPASS** (Cognitive Operations & Mission Planning AI Support System)

**Bridge Description:**
